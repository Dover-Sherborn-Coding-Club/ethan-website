Events

At the Coding Club, you have the opportunity to participate in a variety of events to build experience and add to your applications.

Regular Meetings

By joining us, you gain access to weekly meetings that enhance your networking opportunities and allow you to learn, collaborate, and grow with peers.

USACO

The USA Computing Olympiad (USACO) is the most prestigious computer science event in the US. At the Coding Club, we will guide you through how to participate and train you to be ready to win.

BPA

The Business Professionals of America (BPA) offers competitions across a diverse selection of niches under computer science. Be part of our team to win events with us and reach Nationals!

ACSL

The American Computer Science League (ACSL) is a high-school-level competiton with a variety of computer science tests and challenges. Compete with other schools to score the most points and make it to the Finals!

Hackathons

Hackathons are coding competition that push you to grow, requiring eficient ideation and development with time or other constraints. Learn what to expect and strategies to win!
